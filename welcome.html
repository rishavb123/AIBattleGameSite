Welcome to the NetElixir AI Game

You are a blue box living your peaceful life

Then the purple boxes attacked

The purple boxes have the numbers

And they evolve over time through natural selection to find the best colors, shapes, speeds, and spawn locations to kill you

But you have better technology than them and can utilize your neural networks to help you fight them

You can train them based on your actions so they can automate basic motions

Good Luck!

NOTE: when training a model to shoot hold down the shoot key in the area you want it to shoot

NOTE: in training mode (in configurations) the games you play will not count towards your score. Use this to train a neural network or to practice.
